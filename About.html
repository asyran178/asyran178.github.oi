About Us 

YOUR CHOICE FOR FAST, AFFORDABLE, CLEAN & FRESH LAUNDRY

 
 


Daily Laundry is a self-service laundry or coin-operated laundry that cares about speed, reasonable prices, cleanliness, and freshness in every wash we do. We use high-quality washing machines from the SpeedQueen brand, which is the world's leading supplier of washing machines. This is to ensure a clean laundry type and at the highest level. Apart from that, we provide free Envee brand washing soap and fabric softener so that your clothes stay clean, soft, and have a refreshing fragrance..
